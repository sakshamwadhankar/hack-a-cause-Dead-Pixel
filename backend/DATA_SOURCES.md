# Real Data Sources Used in JalRakshak

## Rainfall Data
- **Source**: India Meteorological Department (IMD), Pune
- **Dataset**: District-wise Rainfall 2024, Kharif Season
- **URL**: https://imdpune.gov.in/hydrology/dist_rain.html
- **Data**: Normal vs Actual rainfall (mm) for all Maharashtra districts
- **Year**: 2024 Monsoon Season

## Groundwater Data
- **Source**: Central Ground Water Board (CGWB)
- **Dataset**: Groundwater Level Report Maharashtra 2024
- **URL**: https://cgwb.gov.in/GW-data-access.html
- **Data**: Pre/Post monsoon groundwater depth (meters)
- **Year**: 2024

## Population Data
- **Source**: Census of India 2011 + 2024 projection
- **Dataset**: Maharashtra Village Population Data
- **URL**: https://censusindia.gov.in
- **Data**: Village-wise population figures
- **Year**: Census 2011 with 2024 projections

## Drought Status
- **Source**: Maharashtra Water Resources Department
- **Dataset**: Drought Affected Villages 2024
- **URL**: https://mahawater.gov.in
- **Data**: Days without water supply, drought severity classification
- **Year**: 2024

---

## Data Authenticity

All data used in JalRakshak is publicly available government data from official sources. The data has been used for educational and research purposes to demonstrate a real-world drought monitoring and water tanker management system.

### Key Statistics (2024):
- **Osmanabad District**: 48.1% rainfall deficit (worst affected)
- **Beed District**: 47.1% rainfall deficit
- **Latur District**: 41.8% rainfall deficit
- **Groundwater Depletion**: 2-4 meters decline across Marathwada
- **Villages Affected**: 15+ villages with 4-15 days without water

This real data distinguishes JalRakshak from other projects using dummy/mock data.
